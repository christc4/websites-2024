> From Old French _mal-_ from the Latin adverb male, from malus

# Prefix

1. evil
    - *mal*icious, *mal*influence
2. poorly, deficiently,
    - *mal*fed, *mal*nourished,
3.  o
    - *mal*igned
4. o
    - *mal*ware

# Affix

Dis*mal*, abyss*mal*

# Notable mals
